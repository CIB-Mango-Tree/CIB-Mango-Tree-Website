Post Time Coordination Low-Hanging Fruit
This test scans for usernames that post at similar times on multiple occasions, revealing signs of possible coordination. It will rank groups of usernames by the number of times that their posts appeared together during the timespan of your dataset, from highest to lowest. You will be able to identify whether sets of usernames are posting together at a frequency that suggests coordination or bot activity, and examine their content.
Required Fields:
Unique Post Username
Unique Post Number
